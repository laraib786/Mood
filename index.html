ABOUT

WELCOME

Woodpecker is a team of professionals in the wood processing and the creation of wooden furniture of the highest class. We are a family of artisans and designers who believe that to make things well, you must put your heart in it. Unique, quality wood products you will keep forever. We are glad to have you around!

Our team takes over everything from an idea and concept developement to realization. We believe in traditions and incorporate them within our innovations. All our projects incorporate a unique artistic image and functional solutions. Client is the soul of the project. Our main goal is to illustrate his/her values and individually through design. Our clients are daring and successful people, businessmen and celebritiesm IT companies and largest banks, media companies and creative agencies, store and restaurant chains, international developing companies, educational centers and public institutions. All our clients have one prevailing trait - they are bold and modern, they like to stay ahead of trendsm and work with the best.
